Hangman

Welcome to Hangman!

In this classic word-guessing game, your mission is to uncover a secret word by guessing one letter at a time. Each correct guess reveals all occurrences of the letter in the word, while each incorrect guess brings you one step closer to being hanged.

To play, simply choose a letter from the English alphabet and input it into the game. If the letter is part of the secret word, it will be revealed in its correct position(s). However, if the letter is not in the word, a part of the hangman will be drawn.

Your goal is to guess all the letters in the word before the entire hangman is drawn. Be careful, as too many incorrect guesses will lead to your defeat!

Remember, every word you encounter is chosen randomly, so each game presents a new challenge. Test your vocabulary, sharpen your guessing skills, and have fun playing Hangman!

Before diving into the game, take a moment to customize your experience by adjusting the settings on the right side of the screen. These settings allow you to tailor the game according to your preferences.
